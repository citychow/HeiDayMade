Story of HeiDayMade

Creation

People talk about mindfulness these days, and I always find peace and joy in the process of crafting. I hope my crafts bring these to you too.

Everything here is imperfect, as I created most of them spontaneously, plus I am a stronger believer that handcrafting is something of this nature. Let's embrace imperfection here and see what it leads to.

In the Gallery, you can find all my handicrafts that are basically anything that came across my mind. 🧠

  • Crochet pieces
  • Beads and accessories
  • Leather works
  • Random works
