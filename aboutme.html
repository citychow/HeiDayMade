HeiDayMade

It is a reflection of my journey as a maker and as an engineer. As a maker, I find joy in transforming raw materials into something meaningful and beautiful. As am engineer, I appreciate the intricacies of design and the importance of functionality. I believe that the two worlds can coexist harmoniously, and that is what I strive to achieve with my work. It started off as a simple idea: to create beautiful, functional pieces that bring joy and warmth into everyday life. I wanted to create a space where people could find unique, handcrafted items that resonate with their own stories and experiences. I wanted to share my love for crafting with others, to inspire them to embrace their own creativity and find beauty in the little things. I wanted to create a community where makers and lovers of handmade could come together, share their stories, and celebrate the art of making.

Hoep my sharing here can give all dear encounters some inspirations and make your life prettier, easier and better!

About Hei

I am an overthinker, a dreamer, and a lover of all things handmade. Behind HeiDayMade is founder Hei Chow, a self-taught artist and maker with a lifelong passion for crafting. Growing up surrounded by her grandmother’s embroidery and her mother’s patchwork quilts, Clara learned early on the beauty of handmade traditions. After years of working in a fast-paced corporate world, she took a leap of faith to follow her creative calling. With a deep appreciation for detail and a love for storytelling through texture and design, Clara pours her soul into every piece she creates. Her mission is simple: to bring warmth, authenticity, and a sense of connection back into the things we choose to keep and gift.
