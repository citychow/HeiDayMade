HeiDayMade

HeiDayMade is a personal project that started as a way to document and share my journey in crafting and web development.

The journey began in late 2024 when I moved from Hong Kong to London; it was a time of transition and self-discovery. As I spent more time reflecting and reorganizing my life, I realized that the deeper I thought, the more craft pieces I created—perhaps because of the peace of mind I found within. Eventually, these crafts began to stack up, creating a new kind of "headache": I needed a way to organize them. That was when I started learning web development, and I found a genuine joy in seeing progress build up, one line of code at a time.

Even with my background as an engineer, it hasn't been an easy journey to re-learn coding, especially in the age of AI where so much can be done with a single prompt. But I hold a strong belief in the saying 萬丈高樓平地起 (a tall building starts from the ground up)—there is nothing that beats a solid foundation. The world is ever-changing, and by learning, we change, we adapt, and we grow. This is just the first step—please stay tuned for my updates.
